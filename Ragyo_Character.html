Ragyo Kiryuin

Diana N.

Satsuki's mother, CEO of REVOCS, leader of the Kiryuin Conglomerate, and the head of Honnouji Academy's board of directors. Her role is fairly minor in the first half of the series until her formal introduction in episode 12, from then more about her is revealed. She plans on enslaving the human race to Life Fibers and has been using REVOCS for this very reason.
